How do I use Peer Review?

The Purpose of Peer Review:

Help fellow support engineers perfect their communication and troubleshooting craft while assisting in the enforcement of departmental initiatives.

The Process:

  1. Click on the "Get Cases" button
  2. On the right, a box will display 10 random cases
  3. Click on a case to review
  4. Read the entire case.
  5. Answer all questions and submit your review
  6. The Support Engineer that was reviewed receives an email with an anonymous review


Note:


Each Support Engineer is required to submit 5 peer reviews by the last day of each month.
